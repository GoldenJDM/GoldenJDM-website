About Me

Why GoldenJDM? JDM refers to authentic Japanese domestic market cars, and Golden symbolizes their golden era — a decade when engineering, beauty, and soul came together. For me, this isn't just a name; it represents the passion I have for mechanics, design, and speed. I love Iran and its rich culture; a land where poetry, history, and emotion can be found in every corner. I'm fascinated by jet aviation and flying through the endless sky, just as much as I love Japanese cars and drifting. The military world and firearms intrigue me — not from a warfare perspective, but through the lens of precision, discipline, and technology. I enjoy helping people, sharing my experiences, and exploring the vast, sometimes unknown world of the internet. I'm drawn to philosophical and romantic poetry because I believe the human soul can be found between the words. And finally, I see coding not merely as a skill, but as an art of creation; an art where every line can make the world — even if just a little — better, more beautiful, and more intelligent.
